üî• Community Guidelines

Help us keep BeardedVibes a safe and enjoyable platform for everyone

üé¨ Content Guidelines

  • Original Content: Only upload content that you own or have permission to share. Do not upload copyrighted material without authorization.
  • Age-Appropriate: All content must be suitable for a general audience. No explicit, adult, or NSFW content is allowed.
  • Quality Standards: Ensure your uploads are of reasonable quality. Avoid uploading spam, duplicates, or intentionally low-quality content.
  • Accurate Titles: Use titles and descriptions that accurately represent your content. Misleading or clickbait titles may be removed.
  • No Malicious Content: Do not upload content containing malware, phishing attempts, or any harmful code.

üö´ Prohibited Content

  • Violence & Gore: No graphic violence, gore, or content promoting violence against individuals or groups.
  • Hate Speech: No content that promotes hatred, discrimination, or harassment based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics.
  • Harassment: No bullying, stalking, doxxing, or targeting individuals with harmful intent.
  • Illegal Activities: No content promoting or depicting illegal activities, including drug use, theft, or fraud.
  • Misinformation: No deliberately false or misleading content that could cause harm.
  • Spam: No repetitive posts, excessive self-promotion, or automated/bot-generated content.

üí¨ Community Behavior

  • Be Respectful: Treat other users with respect. Disagreements are fine, but personal attacks are not.
  • No Impersonation: Do not impersonate other users, staff members, or public figures.
  • Protect Privacy: Do not share personal information about others without their consent.
  • Report Issues: If you see content that violates these guidelines, use the report feature to alert our team.
  • Follow Staff Instructions: Cooperate with moderators and staff members. Their decisions are final.

üë§ Account Guidelines

  • One Account: Each person should only have one account. Multiple accounts may be suspended.
  • Secure Your Account: Keep your login credentials safe and do not share them with others.
  • Appropriate Profiles: Profile pictures, banners, and bios must follow the same content guidelines.
  • No Account Trading: Accounts cannot be sold, bought, or transferred to others.

üö® Reporting & Enforcement

  • How to Report: Use the report button on any video or profile to report violations. Include details about what rule was broken.
  • Review Process: Our team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action within 24-48 hours.
  • Consequences: Violations may result in content removal, warnings, temporary bans, or permanent account suspension depending on severity.
  • Appeals: If you believe an action was taken in error, you may appeal by contacting our staff team.
